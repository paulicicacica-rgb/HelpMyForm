What Is a Medical Card in Ireland?

A medical card gives you free GP visits, free prescriptions, free hospital care, and access to other health services — all at no cost. It's means-tested, which means it depends on your income.

⏱ 4 min read✓ Updated 2026🇮🇪 Ireland

What a medical card covers

🩺

Free GP visits

Unlimited GP consultations at no charge — including home visits and out-of-hours emergency calls.

💊

Free prescriptions

All prescribed medicines on the approved list are free. You pay nothing at the pharmacy.

🏥

Free public hospital care

Inpatient and outpatient hospital treatment in public hospitals is free — no overnight charges, no A&E fees.

🦷

Dental, optical, and aural

Basic dental treatment, eye tests, and hearing services are covered. More complex treatments may have limits.

🚑

Free ambulance service

Emergency ambulance transport is covered under the medical card.

🧒

Maternity and infant care

Antenatal visits, maternity hospital care, and postnatal checks are all covered.

Medical card vs GP visit card

There are two cards. The medical card covers everything. The GP visit card only covers free GP visits — prescriptions and hospital care are still paid.
