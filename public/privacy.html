🔒 Data Protection

How Your Data is Protected

No logins, no tracking, no data stored. Here is exactly what happens when you use HelpMyForm — in plain language.

The short version

✓Your photo is never stored — it is analysed and immediately discarded
✓We never know who you are — no name, email or personal details collected
✓Nothing is shared with anyone — ever
✓No account required to use the service
✓Fully compliant with GDPR and Irish data protection law
📸

Your photo is never stored

When you take a photo of a form, it is sent for analysis and the guidance is returned to your screen. The image is never saved, never stored, and we have no access to it. Once the analysis is complete, the photo is gone.

👤

We don't know who you are

No account is required. No name, email address, phone number or any personal details are collected at any point. You are completely anonymous when you use HelpMyForm.

🔒

Nothing is shared with third parties

Your form content is never sold, shared or passed to any third party for any purpose. The guidance you receive exists only between you and the tool — nothing leaves that interaction.

📵

No tracking or surveillance

We do not use cookies to track you across the web. The only data stored on your device is your language preference and scan count — saved locally on your phone only, never sent to us.

🇮🇪

GDPR compliant

HelpMyForm is designed to be fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Irish data protection law. We collect the minimum data necessary to provide the service — which is essentially nothing that identifies you.

🏥

Safe for vulnerable users

HelpMyForm was built specifically to help immigrants, refugees and people in difficult situations with Irish government forms. We understand the sensitivity of this. Your information is never at risk.

Questions about privacy?

If you have any questions about how HelpMyForm handles data, please get in touch at hello@helpmyform.com — we will respond within 48 hours.
