Rent Supplement Ayrlandka — Hagaha Soomaali

Rent Supplement waa caawinta ku-meel-gaarka ah ee kiraynta oo ay bixiso Waaxda Ilaalinta Bulshada (DSP). Si ka duwan HAP, waxay loogu talagalay dadka oo ku jira xaalad guri oo ku-meel-gaarka ah.

⏱ 7 daqiiqo akhrinta · ✓ La cusboonaysiiyay 2026 · 🇮🇪 Ayrland
Hagaha buuxa ee IngiriisigaHagahan wuxuu bixiyaa macluumaadka muhiimka ah ee Soomaali. Faahfaahin dheeraad ah, eeg hagaha Ingiriisiga ee HelpMyForm.

Rent Supplement la barbardhig HAP

Rent Supplement

  • DSP (Waaxda Ilaalinta Bulshada) ayaa bixisa
  • Ku-meel-gaar — inta badan 18 bilood oo ugu badan
  • Lacagtu waxay kuu timaadaa adiga (kiraystaha) — mulkiilaha kuma tagi
  • Guud ahaan shaqo waqti buuxa uma shaqayn kartid intaad heshid

HAP

  • Golaha maxalligu wuxuu bixiyaa
  • Xalka dheer
  • Lacagtu si toos ah mulkiilaha ayay u tagi
  • Shaqo waqti buuxa waxaad u shaqayn kartaa
  • Liiska hoyga bulshada daftirka ayaa loo baahan yahay

Cidda xaqa u leh

Rent Supplement wuxuu ugu horreyn u taagan yahay dadka ku noolaa kiraysi gaarka ah ugu yaraan 6 ka mid ah 12-kii bilood ee ugu dambeysay oo hadda kiraynta awoodi waayeen shaqo la'aanta ama dakhli hoos u dhac darteed.

Xaaladda Soomaalida

Haddaad dhawaan yimid Ayrlandka oo aadan gaadhinin 6-biloodka kiraynta gaarka ah, Rent Supplement may heli kartid. La xiriir Citizens Information ama adeegyada bulshada maxalliga ah — laga yaabaa inay jiraan qorshayaal taageero oo kale ah.

Sida loo codsado

Codsiga waxaa laga sameeyaa jidheed Supplementary Welfare Allowance (SWA) Officer ee xarunta Intreo maxalliga ah. Online codsasho uma jirto Rent Supplement.

Dukumiintiyada looga baahan yahay

  • Baasaboor ama dukumiinti aqoonsi ah
  • Warqadda xaaladda imigrashanka
  • Nambarka PPS
  • Heshiiska kiraynta hadda jira
  • Cadayn bixintii kiraynta (xisaabta bankiga)
  • Xisaabta bankiga — 3-da bilood ee ugu dambeysay
