Xisaabta Bangiga Ayrlandka — Hagaha Soomaali

Furista xisaabta bangiga Ayrlandka waxay noqon kartaa mid adag, gaar ahaan haddaad dhawaan timid. Bankiyadu waxay adeegsadaan xeerar adag si ay u hor-istaagaan dhaqdhaqaaqa lacagta sharci-darrada ah. Laakiin xalal ayaa jira.

⏱ 7 daqiiqo akhrinta · ✓ La cusboonaysiiyay 2026 · 🇮🇪 Ayrland
Hagaha buuxa ee IngiriisigaHagahan wuxuu bixiyaa macluumaadka muhiimka ah ee Soomaali. Faahfaahin dheeraad ah, eeg hagaha Ingiriisiga ee HelpMyForm.

Halka laga bilaabo

Tallaabo 1 — Revolut ama N26 (maalinta ugu horeysa)

Laga furo telefoonka — baasaboor iyo sawirkaaga. Cadayn cinwaan Irish looma baahna. IBAN ayaad heshaa isla markaaba. Waxay u shaqeysaa lacag-bixinta, lacag-qaadashada online-ka, iyo gaar ahaan lacag-diiwaangelinta ilaha dakhliga.

Tallaabo 2 — An Post Money (toddobaadka 1–2aad)

Waxaa laga furi karaa xafiis kasta oo An Post (boostada Ayrlandka) ah oo leh dukumiinti aqoonsi ah. Cadayn cinwaan Irish looma baahna si la furo. Waxaad heshaa IBAN Irish (IE), kaadhka debit, waxaadna samayn kartaa debit toos ah.

Tallaabo 3 — AIB ama Bank of Ireland (bilaha 2–3aad)

Haddaad leedahay nambarka PPS iyo cadayn cinwaanka (warqadda An Post, warqadda Revenue, ama qaatarka adeegga), fur xisaab oo buuxda AIB ama Bank of Ireland. Kuwaas waa ikhtiyaaradaha ugu fiican ee dheer-fogaanta.

Dukumiintiyada bankiyada waaweyn

Waxa bangiyadu weydiistaan

  • Baasaboor ama dukumiinti aqoonsi ah — warqadda dowladda oo ay ku jirto xaaladda imigrashanka ayaa la aqbali karaa
  • Nambarka PPS — loo baahan yahay bankiyada waaweyn oo dhan
  • Cadayn cinwaan — warqadda An Post, warqadda Revenue, ama warqad shaqaale
Haddaad cadayn cinwaan lahaynHaddaad deganaatid hoy ku-meel-gaar ah ama qoys ayaa kaa marti qaadaya oo aanay qiimaha adeegyada magacaaga ku jirin — bilow An Post Money ama Revolut. Kuwaas cadayn cinwaan uma baahna.

Lacagta loo diro Soomaaliya

Revolut iyo Wise (hore TransferWise) waa ikhtiyaaradaha ugu fiican ee u dirista lacagta Soomaaliya iyo wadamada kale. Qiimaaha beddelka lacagtu waa mid la mid ah bangiyada — khidmadna yaroo laga badan yahay ama bilaash ah (xaddiyada qorshaha bilaashka ah gudahood).

Ka fogow Western Union iyo khidmadaha bedelka lacagta ee jirka ahKhidmadda iyo qiimaaha beddelka lacagtu aad ayay uga xun yihiin Revolut ama Wise. Farqiga oo la xisaabiyo toddobaadkiiba wuxuu noqon karaa mid xooggan.

Haddii la diido codsigaaga

Xaq sharci ah baad u leedahay in aad furto xisaab aasaasiga ah xariiqda lacag-bixinta ee bangiga kasta ee EU. Haddii la diido iyada oo sabab sharci ah la'aanta ah — codso sharaxaad qoraal ah, haddana loo baahdo fur cabasho rasmi ah FSPO (fspo.ie). Adeeggu bilaash buu yahay. Citizens Information sidoo kale waxay bixin karaan taageero bilaash ah.
