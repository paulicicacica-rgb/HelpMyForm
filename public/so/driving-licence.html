Rukhsadda Wadida Ayrlandka — Hagaha Soomaali

Haddaad Soomaaliya ka soo jeedid ama waddan kale oo aan heshiis la lahayn Ayrlandka, waxaad ku heli kartaa rukhsadda wadida Ayrlandka adiga oo kaga gudbaya habka caadiga ah — imtixaanka aragtida, rukhsad barasho, oo imtixaanka waddada.

Rukhsaddaada wadnimada Soomaalida Ayrlandka

Soomaaliya iyo Ayrlandka wax heshiis ah oo toos ah ah kama dhexeeyo, sidaas darteed rukhsadda wadida Soomaalida lama beddeli karo toos. Laakiin haddaad leedahay rukhsad wadnimo oo sax ah oo Soomaalida ka ah, waxaad kaga faa'iidaysan kartaa wadida muddo ku-meel-gaarka ah intaad habka Ayrlandka ka gudbayso.

Waxaad samayn kartaa rukhsaddaada Soomaalida

Rukhsadda wadida oo sax ah oo Soomaalida ka ah waxay kuu ogolaanaysaa wadida Ayrlandka muddo gaaban — guud ahaan ilaa 12 bilood ka hor intaadan noqon deggan joogto ah. Kadibna waa inaad hesho rukhsad Ayrlandka ah.

Habka helitaanka rukhsadda Ayrlandka

  • 1

    Imtixaanka aragtida (Driver Theory Test)

    Imtixaan computer-ka lagu sameeyo oo leh 40 su'aalood oo ku saabsan xeerarka wadnimada Ayrlandka. Qiimaha waa €45. Waxaad diyaargarowga ka samaysan kartaa website-ka theorytest.ie.

  • 2

    Rukhsadda barasho (Learner Permit)

    Imtixaanka aragtida kadib, codso Learner Permit NDLS (ndls.ie). Waxaad u baahan tahay hubinta caafimaadka (foomka D501 oo dhakhtar buuxiyay — qiyaastii €30–€60).

  • 3

    Cashiirrada la leh macallimka (EDT)

    12 cashiirood oo lagu sameeyo macallim barasho rukhsad leh (EDT — Essential Driver Training) ayaa loo baahan yahay imtixaanka waddada ka hor.

  • 4

    Imtixaanka waddada

    Qiimaha waa €85. Dublin iyo magaalooyinka waaweyn — sugitaanku wuxuu noqon karaa 3–6 bilood — dalbasada waqti hore RSA (Road Safety Authority) website-ka.

Farqiga muhiimka ah ee wadnimada Ayrlandka

Ayrlandka, sida UK, gaadhi-mareenku wuxuu ku socdaa dhagta bidix ee wadada. Wareegga ah-dhaarta (steering wheel) waa bidixda. Haddaad caadaysnayd dhagta midig, u baahan tahay wakhti la qabsasho — gaar ahaan wareegyada wareegga ah iyo wadooyinka yaryar ee miyiga.
