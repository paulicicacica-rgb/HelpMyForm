Making Irish bureaucracy easier to navigate

Irish government forms and systems are not designed to be understood on first contact. The language is technical, the processes are opaque, and the consequences of getting something wrong — a missed document, a wrong answer in a field — can mean weeks of delays.

HelpMyForm exists to close that gap. Plain-language guides to every major form and benefit. Guides for the person filling in the form themselves and for the social workers, support workers, and community organisations helping them do it.

Everything here is free. No account. No paywall. The guides stay online and the information stays accurate.
