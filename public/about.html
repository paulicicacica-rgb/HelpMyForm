What is HelpMyForm

A tool that helps people fill in Irish government forms — in their own language.

HelpMyForm is simple. You take a photo of any Irish government form. It reads it and explains every field in plain language — in your language.

No jargon. No legal terms. No confusion. Just clear, step-by-step guidance that tells you exactly what to write and why.

It works with HAP, PPS, Medical Card, IRP, Jobseeker and more. And because it analyses whatever you photograph, it works with any document — a letter from a landlord, a hospital notice, a school communication.

How it works

Open the tool on your phone. Take a photo of the form. Within seconds you receive a plain-language breakdown of every field — what it is asking, what you need to provide, and an example where relevant.

You can also ask follow-up questions in your language if something is still unclear.

No account is required. Nothing is stored. Your photo is analysed and immediately discarded.

For organisations working with immigrants

HelpMyForm is available free of charge to NGOs, charities, community organisations and public bodies working with migrants, refugees and people with limited English.

We provide a dedicated access code for your clients — no setup, no integration, no cost.
