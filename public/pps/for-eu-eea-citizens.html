PPS Number for EU / EEA Citizens in Ireland

As an EU or EEA citizen you have the right to live and work in Ireland without a visa or work permit. Getting a PPS number is straightforward — but you still need the right documents.

⏱ 4 min read✓ Updated 2026🇮🇪 Ireland
Key advantage for EU citizens
No immigration permission required. EU/EEA citizens do not need an IRP card or visa — just valid ID, proof of address, and evidence of why you need the PPS number.

EU / EEA countries covered

🇷🇴 Romania 🇵🇱 Poland 🇵🇹 Portugal 🇱🇹 Lithuania 🇱🇻 Latvia 🇧🇬 Bulgaria 🇭🇺 Hungary 🇸🇰 Slovakia 🇨🇿 Czech Republic 🇮🇹 Italy 🇪🇸 Spain 🇫🇷 France 🇩🇪 Germany 🇳🇴 Norway (EEA) 🇮🇸 Iceland (EEA) + all other EU states

Documents you need

Valid passport or national identity cardOriginal only — photocopies not accepted. Must be valid, not expired.
Proof of address in IrelandUtility bill, bank statement, or official letter dated within the last 3 months at your Irish address.
Evidence of needJob offer letter, employer letter, school acceptance letter, HAP application letter, or other document showing why you need the PPS number now.
No IRP card needed for EU/EEA citizensUnlike non-EU applicants, you don't need an Irish Residence Permit. Your EU passport or ID card is sufficient proof of your right to be in Ireland.
