How Long Does a PPS Number Take in Ireland?

Most people get their PPS number on the same day as their appointment — or within 5 working days by post. Here's what's normal and what causes delays.

⏱ 3 min read✓ Updated 2026🇮🇪 Ireland

Typical processing times

Same day

At the appointment — most common outcome

If your documents are complete and in order, many Intreo offices issue the PPS number on the spot. You leave with it written on a letter.

1–5 days

Posted after the appointment

Some offices process applications centrally and post the number within a few working days. This is normal — not a sign of a problem.

2–4 weeks

Delayed — something needs follow-up

If you haven't received your number after 2 weeks, contact the office where you had your appointment. There may be a document issue or backlog.

Getting a PPS appointment is often the longest waitIn busy areas like Dublin, getting an appointment slot can take 2–4 weeks. The processing after the appointment itself is fast. Book your appointment as early as possible.
