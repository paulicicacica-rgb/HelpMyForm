PPS Number for Students in Ireland

Whether you're an EU student or studying on a Stamp 2 visa, you can get a PPS number in Ireland — but you need the right documents and your college acceptance letter is key.

⏱ 4 min read✓ Updated 2026🇮🇪 Ireland

Can students get a PPS number?

Yes. Both EU students and non-EU students on a Stamp 2 (student permission) can get a PPS number in Ireland. Your college or university acceptance letter is your main evidence of need.

Stamp 2 has work restrictionsNon-EU students on Stamp 2 can work up to 20 hours per week during term time and 40 hours per week during summer and Christmas holidays. Getting a PPS number doesn't change these limits — it just allows you to work legally within them.

Documents needed

PassportValid passport. EU students can use a national identity card instead.
IRP card (non-EU students)Your Stamp 2 IRP card. EU students don't need this.
College acceptance or enrolment letterOfficial letter from your college confirming you are enrolled. This is your evidence of need.
Proof of address in IrelandStudent accommodation letter, utility bill, or letter from your landlord showing your Irish address.
Your college can helpMost Irish colleges have an international student support office. They can provide letters for PPS applications and often know which local Intreo office processes student applications fastest.
